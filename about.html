JavaMobs is a team of developers, designers, and students who love to help people out. From project development to design and marketing, we want to be there for every step along the way.

JavaMobs has one main goal: to create great products that get the job done. We currently have so much fun working on our app RoadAid! Our app will make it easier for you to navigate through road closures in the Metro Vancouver area.

We don't only report road closures, but we also send alerts/notifications of those closures to make you aware in case you were planning on taking a certain route. Plus our app will be accessible on any mobile device and it's very simple enough for anyone to use!
